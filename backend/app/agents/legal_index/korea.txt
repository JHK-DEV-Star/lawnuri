## 1. CIVIL LAW (민사법)

### Civil Act (민법)
- General Provisions (총칙): Legal capacity, Juridical acts, Agency, Prescription
  Art.1~184
- Property Rights (물권): Ownership(Art.211), Possession(Art.192), Mortgage(Art.356)
  Art.185~372
- Obligations (채권):
  - Contracts General: Art.527~692
  - Sale(매매 Art.563), Lease(임대차 Art.618), Mandate(위임 Art.680)
  - Torts/Damages(불법행위): Art.750(general tort), Art.751(non-pecuniary),
    Art.756(employer liability), Art.758(structure liability)
  - Unjust Enrichment(부당이득): Art.741~749
- Family (친족): Marriage(Art.807), Divorce(Art.834), Parental authority(Art.909)
  Art.767~996
- Succession (상속): Inheritance(Art.997), Will(Art.1060), Forced share(Art.1112)
  Art.997~1118

### Civil Procedure Act (민사소송법): Filing suits, Evidence, Judgment
### Civil Execution Act (민사집행법): Enforcement, Seizure, Auction

### Special Civil Laws:
- Housing Lease Protection Act (주택임대차보호법): Tenant protection, Deposit priority
- Commercial Building Lease Protection Act (상가건물임대차보호법)
- Act on Special Cases Concerning Expedition of Legal Proceedings (소액사건심판법)
- Consumer Protection Act (소비자기본법)
- Electronic Commerce Act (전자상거래법): Online transactions, Withdrawal rights
- Product Liability Act (제조물책임법)

## 2. CRIMINAL LAW (형사법)

### Criminal Act (형법)
- General Provisions (총칙): Elements of crime, Justification, Attempt, Complicity
  Art.1~86
- Specific Crimes (각칙):
  - Homicide(살인 Art.250), Injury(상해 Art.257), Assault(폭행 Art.260)
  - Kidnapping(약취유인 Art.287), Rape/Sexual assault(강간 Art.297)
  - Defamation(명예훼손 Art.307), Insult(모욕 Art.311)
  - Fraud(사기 Art.347), Embezzlement(횡령 Art.355), Breach of trust(배임 Art.355②)
  - Theft(절도 Art.329), Robbery(강도 Art.333)
  - Arson(방화 Art.164), Trespass(주거침입 Art.319)
  - Bribery(뇌물 Art.129), Abuse of authority(직권남용 Art.123)
  - Forgery(문서위조 Art.231)

### Criminal Procedure Act (형사소송법): Investigation, Prosecution, Trial
### Special Criminal Laws:
- Act on Special Cases Concerning Punishment of Sexual Crimes (성폭력처벌법)
- Act on Prevention of Domestic Violence (가정폭력처벌법)
- Stalking Punishment Act (스토킹처벌법)
- Act on Aggravated Punishment of Specific Crimes (특정범죄가중처벌법)
- Act on Punishment of Narcotics (마약류관리법)
- Road Traffic Act (도로교통법): DUI, Traffic violations

## 3. COMMERCIAL LAW (상사법)

### Commercial Act (상법)
- Commercial Transactions: Art.46~168
- Companies (회사):
  - Stock corporation(주식회사 Art.288), LLC(유한회사 Art.543)
  - Directors' duties(Art.382), Shareholders' rights(Art.363)
  - M&A, Dissolution
- Insurance (보험): Art.638~739
- Maritime (해상): Art.740~932

### Special Commercial Laws:
- Financial Investment Services Act (자본시장법): Securities, Investment
- Fair Trade Act (공정거래법): Monopoly, Unfair practices
- Bankruptcy Act (채무자회생법): Rehabilitation, Bankruptcy
- Check/Bill Act (어음법, 수표법)
